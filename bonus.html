Fictional Character Title

Lewis Navy the Leopard

Lewis is a Navy-colored leopard that has exceptional skills in the water. He brings
the life of leopards to the ocean and makes several friends with leopard seals. They teach him
life lessons from the ocean that he takes back to the other leopards on land.

Games he plays:

  1. Underwater tag
  2. Ships and Cats
  3. Diver's Hunt
