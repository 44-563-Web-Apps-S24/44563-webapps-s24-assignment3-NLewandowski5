My Favorite Restaurant: Raising Cane's

I love Raising Cane's for several reasons. First, their chicken has an edge over other chicken.
I don't know how to describe it, but something about the flavor and crunch makes it unbelievably good.
There signature bread and sauce top it all off. There is not another chicken restaurant where I consistently
get extra food more than Cane's.
